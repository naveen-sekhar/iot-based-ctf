SynCore: The Final Memory

The year is 2025. Neel Verma, the visionary behind SynCore—the most advanced AI-powered smart home system—was found dead in his villa. The smart home remains operational, recording odd data moments before his mysterious death. You’ve been granted access to investigate. Your job: follow the IoT trails and identify the killer.

Your Mission

  • Explore different smart systems within the home.
  • Uncover all 6 hidden clues (formatted as clue{...}).
  • Input clues on the clue submission page.
  • Make your final accusation from 3 suspects.

Game Structure

  • 8 total pages, some hidden
  • Use IDOR (Insecure Direct Object Reference) to navigate manually via URL changes (e.g., mirror.html, health.html)
  • No "Next" buttons – only clues and logic!

Known IoT Devices in Neel Verma's Smart Home

  • Smart Stove
  • Smart Lock
  • Smart Mirror
  • Motion Sensor (Pantry)
  • Smart Lights
  • Health Monitor

Suspects

  • Maya Sinha: Ex-girlfriend & SynCore Co-founder
  • Rian Kapoor: Investor
  • Sonal Verma: Sister

Rules

  • No login required. Entire game is browser-based.
  • Use browser inspect tools, hover actions, and common sense.
  • No brute force or hacking — just observation and logic.
  • Do not refresh your page once you start.

Hint: Begin with kitchen.html.
